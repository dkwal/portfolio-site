Who I am

I am a Bay Area native that loves snowboarding, all things games, and most importantly, coding. Whether it be finding the best strategy to win a game or finding the optimal solution to a LeetCode problem, I am always up for a challenge.

My curiosity for coding began in the games industry where I worked as a QA test analyst. I enjoyed finding bugs and figuring out what caused them and how to reproduce them, but ultimately I became more curious about the code that made it all work to begin with. My curiosity eventually led me to the App Academy full stack software engineering boot camp in San Francisco where I learned to code projects and games of my own.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy board game nights with friends, taking weekend trips to Lake Tahoe, and going to live concerts. My favorite video game is Apex Legends, which I got to work on while I worked at Respawn Entertainment.
